SWITCH OFF

Grid of squares. Aim of the game is to switch off all the lights.

Must have a clickable grid 
Board must load with some pre-determined squares on the grid displayed as lit.
Each click will toggle the style of the clicked element - and also the styles of the four immediately abutting elements.
After each click will check to determine if a win has been achieved
Must display the number of clicks used in that round
Once a round has been completed, after a short delay will load the next round
